I was born in Kharkiv, Ukraine, in 1995.

I had always been fascinated by technology, whether it was cars, airplanes, or computers, since my early years.

After graduating from high school, I became a student at the National University of Urban Economy, where I earned both my bachelor's and master's degrees in Management (hospitality).

In the same year that I graduated from university (2019), I also got married. Unfortunately, I had to leave my homeland just one day before the war started in 2022, and I couldn't go back.

That's how I ended up in the USA.
